Cantaloupe Photography

Born in Toronto, Canada. Founder Nikki Persaud first picked up a camera at the early age of 15 - influenced by magazine articles, artistic editing and the school's yearbook team.

Growing alongside the rise of social media and enhancing techniques enabled Nikki to reach into an international industry. After graduating university with a biology degree, she worked in the technology industry heavily associated with marketing and over time naturally transitioned into nature and landscape photography. A couple of years later, she embarked on her first journey as a homeowner near the US-Canada border in Windsor.

Nikki enjoys creating captivating artistic representations of subjects that invoke emotions and interest. She is inspired by strong-minded women, unique beauty and timeless styles.

Nikki currently resides in Windsor, Ontario. She regularly travels for assignments, working between the Greater Toronto Area and abroad.
